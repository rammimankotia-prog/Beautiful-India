Overview

Embark on a breathtaking journey through the majestic Swiss Alps. Experience the thrill of high-altitude trains, stunning glacier views, and the charm of traditional mountain villages. This 7-day adventure offers the perfect blend of pristine nature and luxurious comfort.

From the cosmopolitan streets of Zurich to the diplomatic hub of Geneva, this curated tour takes you through the heart of Switzerland. You'll ride the legendary Glacier Express, hike easy but scenic trails around Zermatt, and wake up to views of the Matterhorn. Hand-picked boutique accommodations ensure you rest comfortably after days filled with alpine exploration.

Itinerary

Day 1: Welcome to Zurich

Arrive in Zurich. Meet your guide and fellow travelers for a welcome dinner in the old town. Evening stroll along Lake Zurich.

Day 2: Lucerne & Mount Pilatus

Morning train to Lucerne. Take the steepest cogwheel railway in the world up to Mount Pilatus. Afternoon at leisure exploring Lucerne's Chapel Bridge.

Day 3: The GoldenPass Line to Interlaken

Scenic panoramic train ride to Interlaken. Optional afternoon paragliding or boat cruise on Lake Thun.
