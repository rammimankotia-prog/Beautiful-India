Our Story

It started with a single backpack and a curiosity that knew no borders. In the late 90s, our founder, Julian Thorne, realized that the travel industry was losing its soul to mass-market packages.

He envisioned a platform that would bridge the gap between luxury comfort and raw, authentic experience. Today, Wanderlust Explorer Pro is a global community of curators, explorers, and local experts dedicated to the path less traveled.

Our mission is simple: to manage world-class tours that respect local cultures and create lifelong memories for the discerning traveler.
